How it works

A straightforward process, handled with care at every step.

  1. Book an appointment →

    Choose your package, vehicle size, and add-ons. Availability and duration are shown at booking.

    A ${{ site.deposit_amt }} deposit is collected at booking.

  2. Drop-off

    Arrive at your scheduled time. We’ll do a quick walk-around and confirm requested services.

    Please limit the amount of personal items in your vehicle. While we do our best to store away your items carefully during the cleaning process, this can greatly slow down our process.

  3. Detailing in progress

    We’ll text/call if anything changes. Add-ons can be approved during the service.

  4. Pick-up

    We’ll notify you 30 minutes before it’s ready. We will do a final review of the vehicle at pickup and you’re good to go.
    Aftercare tips available upon request.
